About Midnight Cravings

Midnight Cravings is a simple and creative cooking website designed to help people prepare delicious meals quickly and easily. This website focuses on vegetarian recipes that are perfect for students and beginners.

Whether you are hungry at midnight or just want to try something new, this platform provides a variety of recipes including quick meals, main courses, desserts, beverages, and snacks.

This project was created as part of a web development assignment and aims to deliver a clean, modern, and user-friendly experience.
