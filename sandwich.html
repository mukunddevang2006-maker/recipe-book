Grilled Sandwich

⏱ 10 mins 🔥 Easy

Ingredients

  • 4 bread slices
  • Butter
  • Cheese slices
  • Tomato, cucumber
  • Salt & pepper

Steps

  1. Spread butter on bread
  2. Add vegetables and cheese
  3. Season with salt and pepper
  4. Grill until golden brown
  5. Serve hot
