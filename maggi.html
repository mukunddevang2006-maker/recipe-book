Masala Maggi

⏱ 5 mins 🔥 Easy

Ingredients

  • 1 packet Maggi noodles
  • 1.5 cups water
  • Maggi tastemaker
  • 1 chopped onion
  • 1 chopped tomato
  • Green chilli (optional)

Steps

  1. Boil water in a pan
  2. Add vegetables and cook for 2 minutes
  3. Add Maggi noodles
  4. Add tastemaker
  5. Cook for 2-3 minutes and serve hot
