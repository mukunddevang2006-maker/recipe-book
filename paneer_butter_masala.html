Paneer Butter Masala

⏱ 30 mins 🔥 Medium

Ingredients

  • 200g paneer
  • 2 tomatoes
  • 1 onion
  • Butter & cream
  • Spices

Steps

  1. Cook onion and tomato
  2. Blend into smooth gravy
  3. Add butter and spices
  4. Add paneer cubes
  5. Cook for 10 mins and serve
