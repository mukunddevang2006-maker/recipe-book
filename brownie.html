Chocolate Brownie

⏱ 40 mins 🔥 Medium

Ingredients

  • 1 cup flour
  • 1/2 cup cocoa powder
  • 1 cup sugar
  • Butter
  • 2 eggs

Steps

  1. Mix dry ingredients
  2. Add butter and eggs
  3. Pour batter into tray
  4. Bake for 25-30 minutes
  5. Cool and serve
